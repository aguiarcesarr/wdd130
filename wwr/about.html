History

Whitewater Rafting Co. was founded in 1978 by two thrill-seeking brothers, Jake and Sam Rivers, after a legendary (and slightly exaggerated) descent of the untamed Thunder Gorge. Rumor has it they navigated the entire stretch with nothing but a homemade raft, a stray dog named Paddles, and an unshakable love for adventure. Over the years, Whitewater Rafting has grown into a premier rafting company, offering guided tours through some of the wildest rapids in the country. While Paddles has long since retired, his adventurous spirit lives on in every ride. Today, we continue the tradition of daring exploration, expert guiding, and unforgettable experiences—minus the homemade rafts.
